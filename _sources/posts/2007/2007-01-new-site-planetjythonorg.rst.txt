New site: planet.jython.org
===========================

*This was originally posted on blogger* `here <https://pyfound.blogspot.com/2007/01/new-site-planetjythonorg.html>`_.

Introducing a new PSF site: `planet.jython.org <http://planet.jython.org>`_,
which collects posts by Jython users and developers. If your weblog belongs on
Planet Jython, please write to <webmaster at python.org>.
