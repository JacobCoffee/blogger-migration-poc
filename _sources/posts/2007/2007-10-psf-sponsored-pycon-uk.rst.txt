PSF Sponsors New PyCons
=======================

*This was originally posted on blogger* `here <https://pyfound.blogspot.com/2007/10/psf-sponsored-pycon-uk.html>`_.

The PSF was one of the sponsors of `PyCon UK <http://www.pyconuk.org/>`_, which
took place September 8th and 9th in Birmingham. $2,500 were donated. About 190
attended the conference. The next PyCon UK will be held the 12th to 14th
September 2008 also in Birmingham.

The PSF was also a Gold Sponsor for `PyCon Italia <http://www.pycon.it/>`_,
which took place June 9th and 10th in Firenze. 1,500 Euros were donated. Our
three free passes were donated to students and others that needed the
financial assistance. About 200 attendees were at the conference. The 2008
conference has not yet been scheduled.
