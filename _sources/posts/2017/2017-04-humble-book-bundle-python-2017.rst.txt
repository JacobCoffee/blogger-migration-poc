Pay What You Want for "The Humble Book Bundle: Python" and Benefit the PSF
==========================================================================

*This was originally posted on blogger* `here <https://pyfound.blogspot.com/2017/04/humble-book-bundle-python-2017.html>`_.

Pay what you want for a stack of Python ebooks from No Starch Press, and
decide what portion goes to the PSF. This deal is presented by `Humble
Bundle <https://humblebundle.com/>`_, which sells ebooks and games to raise
money for nonprofits. When you buy a bundle you choose how much to pay, and
how the money is divided among the creators, Humble Bundle, Inc., and the
nonprofit organization.  
  
The `Humble Book Bundle: Python <https://www.humblebundle.com/books/python-
book-bundle>`_ is available now through April 19th. Pay a dollar or more for
these three books:
