PyCon UK Registration Open
==========================

*This was originally posted on blogger* `here <https://pyfound.blogspot.com/2008/08/pycon-uk-registration-open.html>`_.

PyCon UK takes place in Birmingham UK from 12th to 14th September 2008. A full
programme includes a day of tutorials; bookings are being taken at
<http://www.pyconuk.org/booking.html>. The early bird rate has been extended
to 11th August.

There will be plenary talks by Mark Shuttleworth, Ted Leung and Raymond
Hettinger.

You can see a list of already accepted talks at
<http://pyconuk.org/talk_abstracts.html> and a schedule for the tutorial day
is at <http://pyconuk.org/timetable.html>
