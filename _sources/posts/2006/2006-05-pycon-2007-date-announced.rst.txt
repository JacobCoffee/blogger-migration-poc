PyCon 2007 date announced
=========================

*This was originally posted on blogger* `here <https://pyfound.blogspot.com/2006/05/pycon-2007-date-announced.html>`_.

The planning committee has set the date for the next PyCon conference: `PyCon
2007 <http://us.pycon.org/TX2007/>`_ will be February 23 to 25 2007, in Addison,
Texas.
