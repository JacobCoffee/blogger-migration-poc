If you're a voting member of the Python Software Foundation, then on May 20
you were emailed a ballot to vote for this year's Board of Directors. The
voting booths close at the end of May 30, `Anywhere on
Earth <http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anywhere_on_Earth>`_, so please get your
votes in!  
  
Who is a voting member? Details of membership levels and voter registration,
along with the list of candidates for this year's board, are on the PSF wiki:  

  

*`Voting, Election Process, and Candidates'
Statements <https://wiki.python.org/moin/PythonSoftwareFoundation/BoardCandidates2016>`_*
