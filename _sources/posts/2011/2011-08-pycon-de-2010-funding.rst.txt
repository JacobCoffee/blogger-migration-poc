PyCon DE 2011 Funding
=====================

*This was originally posted on blogger* `here <https://pyfound.blogspot.com/2011/08/pycon-de-2010-funding.html>`_.

The PSF Board of Directors has awarded a $1500 USD grant to sponsor `PyCon DE
2011 <http://de.pycon.org/2011/home/>`_.

Date and Location
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

PyCon DE 2011 will be held from October 4th to 9th in Leipzig, Germany. The
conference will be held at the `Leipziger Kubus <http://leipziger-kubus.de/>`_.

PyCon DE
^^^^^^^^

PyCon DE 2011 is the first PyCon in the German-speaking countries. It consist
of one day of 12 high-quality tutorials, three days of more than 60 talks, and
two days of sprints. The topics reflect the wide usage of Python, including
web development, science and engineering, testing, Python in business,
databases, financial engineering, code quality and much more. The organizers
are expecting between 250 and 300 attendees from the excited German-speaking
Python community.

More Information
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

For more details, refer to the `PyCon DE 2011 <http://de.pycon.org/2011/home/>`_
website.
