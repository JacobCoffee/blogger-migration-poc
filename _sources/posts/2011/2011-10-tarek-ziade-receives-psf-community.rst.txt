Tarek Ziadé Receives PSF Community Service Award
================================================

*This was originally posted on blogger* `here <https://pyfound.blogspot.com/2011/10/tarek-ziade-receives-psf-community.html>`_.

The Python Software Foundation has presented a `Community Service
Award <http://www.python.org/community/awards/psf-awards/>`_ for the second
quarter of 2011 to `Tarek Ziadé <http://ziade.org/>`_ for his hard work leading
the effort to revamp Python's packaging tools.

The Python Software Foundation is pleased to recognize Tarek's contributions
to the community.
