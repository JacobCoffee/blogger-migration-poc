Thinking about running for the Python Software Foundation Board of Directors? Let’s talk!
=========================================================================================

*This was originally posted on blogger* `here <https://pyfound.blogspot.com/2023/05/thinking-about-running-for-python.html>`_.

This year’s Board Election Nomination period is `opening
tomorrow <https://pyfound.blogspot.com/2023/05/psf-board-election-dates-
for-2023.html>`_. Current board members want to share what being on the board is
like and are making themselves available to answer all your questions about
responsibilities, activities and time commitments via online chat. Please come
join us `on Slack <https://www.python.org/join-psf-board-slack>`_ anytime in
June to talk with us about being on the PSF board.  
  
Board Election Timeline:  

  * Nominations are open, Thursday, June 1st, 2:00 pm UTC
  * Board Director Nomination cut-off: Thursday, June 15, 11:59 pm UTC
  * Voter application cut-off date: Thursday, June 15, 11:59 pm UTC
  * Announce candidates: Friday, June 16th
  * Voting start date: Tuesday, June 20, 12:01 am UTC
  * Voting end date: Friday, June 30, 11:59 pm UTC

Not sure what UTC is for you locally? Check
`here <https://savvytime.com/converter/utc>`_!  
  
Nominations will be accepted
`here <https://www.python.org/nominations/elections/>`_. (Note: you will need to
sign into or create your python.org user account first). Learn more about
membership `here <https://www.python.org/psf/membership/>`_ or if you have
questions about membership or nominations please email `psf-
elections@python.org <mailto:psf-elections@python.org>`_In addition to
`Slack <http://psf-board.slack.com>`_ you are welcome to join the discussion
about the PSF Board election on `our
forum <https://discuss.python.org/c/python-software-foundation/9>`_.

  
Also, you can see your membership record and status on
`psfmember.org <https://psfmember.org/user-information>`_. If you are a voting-
eligible member and do not already have a login there, please `sign
up <https://psfmember.org/wp-login.php?action=register>`_ and then email `psf-
donations@python.org <mailto:psf-donations@python.org>`_ so we can link your
membership to your account.
